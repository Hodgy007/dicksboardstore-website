Engineered for the discerning expert skier, the Salomon S/Pro 120 GW ski boot delivers an unparalleled blend of performance, comfort, and precision. This high-performance boot features a customisable shell and liner, allowing for a truly personalised fit right out of the box.

Salomon's Coreframe 360° construction with Sense Amplifier ensures exceptional power transmission and responsiveness, giving you total control in every turn. The new Sense Strap enhances rebound and provides a progressive flex, while the Custom Shell HD process and My CustomFit 3D Seamless Pro liner mean we can mould the boot to your exact foot shape — eliminating pressure points and maximising comfort without sacrificing performance.

Pre-mounted GripWalk (GW) soles offer improved walking comfort and enhanced grip, making transitions in the resort much easier. Experience a new standard of on-snow performance and off-snow convenience with the S/Pro 120 GW.
