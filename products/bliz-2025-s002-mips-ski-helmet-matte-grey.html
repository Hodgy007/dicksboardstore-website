Bliz 2025 S002 MIPS Ski Helmet - Matte Grey

£102

The Bliz S002 MIPS is a sleek, low-profile ski and snowboard helmet designed for both piste and freeride. It features a lightweight in-mold construction with an EPS foam core and ABS shell for solid impact protection. The built-in MIPS system helps reduce rotational forces during angled impacts, improving safety. For comfort, it has an adjustable Air Flow ventilation system to regulate temperature, and a Retention Fit System plus a padded chin strap to ensure a secure, personalized fit. Additional touches include soft ear pads, a goggle clip at the back, and a microfiber carrying pouch.
