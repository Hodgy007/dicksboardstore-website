Expert Boot Fitting

At Dick's Board Store, our boot fitting service is backed by over 35 years of dedicated expertise, ensuring you get the perfect fit for ultimate comfort and performance on the slopes. We utilise advanced techniques, including custom heat moulding of liners and shells, to perfectly sculpt the boot to your unique foot anatomy.

Our precise pressure mapping technology identifies pressure points, allowing us to make targeted adjustments for a truly bespoke fit that eliminates discomfort and enhances control — whether you're a first-timer or an Olympic hopeful.

What's Included

  • Foot measurement and assessment (length, width, arch)
  • Pressure mapping scan to identify problem areas
  • Shell fitting — trying multiple brands and lasts
  • Custom heat moulding of liner to your foot shape
  • Shell modification if required (spot heating/punching)
  • Custom footbed creation for arch support
  • Forward lean and canting adjustment
  • Walk-out test and final refinements
Session length: 1–2 hours. Appointment essential. Boot fitting fee from £30 (redeemable against boot purchase).

Ski & Snowboard Servicing

Keep your skis in peak condition with our professional in-house workshop at Dick's Board Store, equipped to handle all your maintenance needs. Our skilled technicians provide expert edge sharpening for optimal grip and carving, alongside comprehensive base repairs to address any gouges or damage.

We finish every service with a high-quality waxing treatment, ensuring smooth gliding and protecting your bases, so you're always ready for the mountain. Drop off and collect in store — typically ready within 24–48 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Professional boot fitting is crucial for comfort, performance, and injury prevention. A properly fitted boot ensures efficient power transfer to your skis, reduces pressure points, and prevents fatigue — allowing you to ski longer and better with enhanced control. Without proper fitting, even the most expensive boot can cause pain and reduce performance significantly.
A comprehensive boot fitting session typically takes between 1 to 2 hours. This allows our expert fitters ample time for foot assessment, trying various models, heat moulding processes, and any necessary adjustments to achieve the perfect personalised fit. We recommend booking in advance to ensure we have dedicated time for you.
The frequency depends on how often you ski and in what conditions. For casual skiers, a full service at the beginning and end of the season is usually sufficient. More frequent skiers, or those tackling aggressive terrain, might benefit from waxing every 3–5 ski days and edge tunes as needed. Rocky or icy conditions are hardest on edges and bases — we can advise on inspecting your own equipment between services.
Our basic ski service (£25) includes a thorough base clean, professional edge sharpening and deburring, minor base repairs (filling small scratches), and a hot wax application using high-quality universal wax to ensure optimal glide and protection. For more significant damage or a full grind, we'd recommend our Full Service at £45.
