Our Story

Independent. Passionate. Sheffield.

Born from a pure love of the mountains and the freedom of the snow, Dick's Board Store has been Sheffield's independent spirit for 35 years. In 1989, founder Dick Hartley opened a small shop on Infirmary Road with one mission: to give every skier and snowboarder in South Yorkshire access to the same level of expertise and premium gear that professional athletes relied on.

We're more than just a shop — we're a community hub where passion meets unparalleled expertise, ensuring every shredder finds their perfect ride. Our legacy is built on honest advice, genuine service, and the thrill of helping you conquer every piste and powder field.

We remain fiercely independent. We're not owned by a chain or investor. Every product we stock has been tested and approved by our team, every brand relationship built on trust, and every customer interaction driven by a genuine love of winter sports.

Meet the Team

Passionate mountain people — every single one of us

Dick Hartley
Founder & Head Boot Fitter

35 years behind the boot bench. Dick has fitted boots for everyone from first-timers to GB Ski Team members. Obsessed with getting the perfect fit.

Jess Collins
Senior Boot Fitter & Snowboard Specialist

Former competitive snowboarder with 12 years at Dick's. Jess knows every board in the shop intimately and is our go-to for all things snowboarding.

Tom Bradley
Head Technician & Workshop Manager

Tom has serviced over 10,000 pairs of skis and boards in his career. If it's broken, damaged, or needs a tune, Tom will have it running like new.

Amy Xu
Junior Boot Fitter & Clothing Buyer

Three seasons in Chamonix gave Amy an encyclopaedic knowledge of ski outerwear. She curates all our clothing and accessories lines each season.
